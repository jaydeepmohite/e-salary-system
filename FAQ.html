Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)

  1. Is it necessary to register school before teacher registration ?

    2. Ans :- Ofcourse . It is necessary for school to register. The teacher is verified with the school before school registration.
  2. Is it possible for me to see previous salary transfer status.

    3. Ans :-Yes . It is possible to see the previous salary transfer status.Login as a teacher and go to salary transfer TAB.
  3. Can I apply for the PF loans?

    4. Ans :- No . Sorry .The current system does not provide this feature ,but will be added soon.
  4. Is it possible for me to change my E-mail ID ??

    5. Ans :- Yes. It is possible,but the updation may take upto 24 hours.
  5. What if my School Id/Teacher ID is not registered?

    6. Ans :- In that case please contact your school.
