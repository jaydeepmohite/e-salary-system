About Us

According To The Government Of Maharashtra, Education Unit, Decision No. SSN | 1867 | 76142 G. Dated 29/05/1973, All The Government Aided, Non-Government School Teaching And Non-Teaching Staff Salaries And Allowances Are To Be Given Through Government Banks And For That The Payroll And E-Salary Systems Have Been Established.
