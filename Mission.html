Mission

From Education officer's desk,

Out mission is to provide a platform to make a process of salary transform of the government school teachers residing in Pune much easier,simplier,efficient and a quick process.
The website provides the school with the registration option which allows itself to register and create a profile.Once the profile is created the school can login and can view it's profile.The school then is provided with various option such as to submit staff details,add a new teacher,delete an existing teacher and to upload the salary database every month.
Once the school is registered ,then teachers belonging to that school can also register and create their own profile.The teacher ,then can login and is provided with the features such as to view the payroll slip of current month and to check the salary status of the current month as well as the previous months.
In short,the mission of our website is to make the overall process of salary transaction simple and fulfill our moto which is

"...Better solution for salary transfer"
